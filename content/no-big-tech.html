No Big Tech

Part of my contrarian atittued towards everything has always drawn me to find ways in which I can find alternatives to the norm, and being a tech nerd, that has often meant how to avoid using big tech and relying only on open source.

I would be lying though if I said that I have always been an open-source user. There have been several spots on my life where I have sucumbeed to the ease of use of big tech, and deemed the effort requried to live an open-source life too much of a hasle. I'm in the process of going back to open-source and hopefully for the last time. This posts aims to keep track of all the things that I'm doing and how to implement them. In a way, this is just a public to-do list to keep me honest.
